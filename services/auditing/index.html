Website Audit

If your website:

  • does not contribute to the growth of sales of your products or services;
  • is not indexed in search engines for key terms;
  • poorly positions your company;
  • is not accessible during presentations;

CONSIDER WEBSITE AUDIT!

The main task of a website audit is to identify the strengths and weaknesses of the project's functioning and to suggest improvements. Website audit is not an assessment of design, code quality, support level, or search engine results. It is an analysis of the marketing component of project development, formulation of correct functioning tasks based on business specifics.

Are you sure your website is working as efficiently as possible? Can you increase targeted traffic, boost sales through the website, and gain loyal users or brand recognition? Audit provides answers to these questions.

WEBSITE AUDIT CONSISTS OF THE FOLLOWING COMPONENTS:

  • analysis of domain name and hosting;
  • analysis of project structure, usability, and navigation;
  • analysis of website content placement (text and images);
  • analysis of search engine optimization and website promotion.

TERMS AND PAYMENT

The cost of the audit starts from 75 USD. The audit includes a detailed electronic and printed report on the strengths and weaknesses of the resource. Depending on the complexity of the project and the audit tasks set, the completion period from the moment of signing the contract to its delivery can range from 5 to 15 business days.
