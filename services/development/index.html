Why do you need a corporate website?

"If a company is not on the Internet, it doesn't exist" — this may seem strange and exaggerated, but this is how business works today. Over the past 5 years, the shift of consumers to the online world has become more noticeable. Even before going to an auto repair shop, the car owner will definitely google a couple of local companies. And if the search engine does not provide him with information about your auto repair shop, then he simply won't turn to it. That is why it is so important to have a corporate website.

Corporate website — the online office of your business

A corporate website is an internet resource dedicated to a specific company. It contains information about its activities, contact details, a list of basic services, development history, and everything that may be useful to a potential consumer. Essentially, a corporate website is a full-fledged office of the company. But located on the internet.

A vivid example of a corporate website is the NASA portal. Nothing is sold here. But all the information about the US government agency is presented here. Corporate websites also include platforms like Apple, Prada, and others.

The resource needed for your business

Creating a corporate website will be relevant for any company interested in:

  • increasing profits;
  • improving image;
  • increasing recognition.

Developing a corporate resource is an integral part of launching and developing a business. This applies to both international brands (such as Apple) and regional enterprises (such as a small printing house in Kyiv). For business to grow and be profitable, it must be present on the internet.

Creating a corporate website will be useful for hotels and beauty salons, real estate agencies and notarial services, construction companies, and wholesale suppliers of industrial equipment both in Kyiv and other cities. Its development is necessary if you do not sell goods at retail (in which case an online store would be more relevant) but provide certain services to consumers.

Functional capabilities of corporate websites

A corporate website plays one of the leading roles in promoting the entire business. This is explained by its wide functional capabilities:

1
Creating the right image. A corporate website builds trust with consumers, making the brand more prestigious and reliable in their eyes.
2
Customer acquisition. A business website allows you to promote online, attracting new customers and partners for cooperation.
3
Expertise formation. Through useful articles in a blog, you can significantly increase the level of expertise and gain trust from customers.
4
Mass information dissemination. Through the website, you can announce important changes in the company, current promotions, and more.
5
Feedback. Users can quickly find contact information or contact a manager to discuss cooperation through chat.
6
Increase in sales. Internet advertising, SEO, and quality content contribute to increased direct sales from the website.
