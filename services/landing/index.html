How Landing Page Creation Can Help

Today, a landing page is one of the most effective tools for promoting and developing a business, increasing sales. A landing page is a "landing" web page, a one-page site that encourages visitors to take some specific targeted action, such as ordering a product/service, registering, or subscribing. Landing pages are used to collect user data, announce events, and sell a specific service or product.

Arguments for Developing a Landing Page

Sales made exclusively offline often hinder a company's transition to a new stage of development. The main reason lies in the management's disregard for such a channel as the internet. This usually applies to small businesses that operate on the principle: if something sells well "from the counter," why bother going online.

Another part of entrepreneurs tries to sell their products through free services (like Olx) or offers their goods through marketplaces (for example, Rozetka). Undoubtedly, this increases the audience and recognition of the product. But behind the screen of free sales or through marketplaces hide their nuances, including paid advertising or the inability to name the store/product by its name.

In addition, in various industries, there are niche products/services that are not quite suitable for selling "from the counter": to order, a presentation and demonstration of their best qualities are required.

For these cases, the solution will be to develop a website like a landing page - capable of giving a serious boost to sales growth. It can work in parallel with other channels, and often more effectively in combination with other sources of online advertising.

Ordering the creation of a landing page at a reasonable price allows you to advertise even the most specific product or service. Setting up and connecting additional Google Adwords advertising will allow you to quickly increase sales volumes, brand/company recognition. Also, landing page development can be used for traffic from social networks: a link to the service website (product, brand) in profiles statistically increases trust in the product.

How to Make a Landing Page Effective

In order to understand whether a target single-page website is needed for the company and how to approach the development process to maximize the effectiveness of the landing page, it is necessary to consider:

  • what can be said about the product/service with the help of the landing page;
  • what unique properties of the product can be useful for the client, what features of the product and requests of the target audience can be covered with its help;
  • how best to use or apply it;
  • prices, discounts, payment features, delivery.

It is highly desirable to have high-quality photos of the product/service and a video guide - these are real must-haves for creating a landing page.
